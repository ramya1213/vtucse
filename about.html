About App

VTU CS APP is developed to make the academic syllabus details of Visvesvaraya Technological University,Belgaum available for students. The speciality of this app is that it provides notes, syllabus copies & Lab Manuals in only one site .We assure the user that, in the next releases & updates of our app we'll try to add more information with the enhanced rich user interface.

App contains following information:
* Complete syllabus of B.E.(CSE) & 1st year syllabus(All Branches) & Question Papers are provided.
* Second, Third & Fourth year's syllabus Copies,Notes,Manuals are also provided.
